About Me

Neelesh K Shukla is a Principal Applied Scientist at Oracle Generative AI, specializing in Generative AI, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), and intelligent enterprise AI systems. With over 14 years of experience spanning scientific research, applied AI, and large-scale production systems, his work focuses on advancing the intersection of AI innovation and enterprise-scale deployment.

At Oracle, he leads applied science initiatives for Generative AI and Advanced AI products, driving the development of enterprise-grade AI systems grounded in retrieval, reasoning, orchestration, and rigorous evaluation methodologies. His work emphasizes building reliable, scalable, and production-ready AI architectures capable of operating effectively under real-world complexity, scale, and business constraints.

Over the course of his career, Neelesh has led and contributed to initiatives involving Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), NL2SQL systems, semantic retrieval architectures, multilingual and multimodal AI, document intelligence, and agentic AI systems. His research interests extend to evaluation-first AI engineering, knowledge-centric AI architectures, conversation memory systems, and robust AI reasoning frameworks.

Prior to Oracle, he served as Senior Manager and Data Scientist at State Street, where he established and led AI Research and Innovation initiatives focused on accelerating the adoption of Generative AI within the financial services domain. He has also worked with organizations including Landis+Gyr and contributed to research-oriented initiatives through the OSINT Lab at IIT Guwahati.

Neelesh holds an M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Guwahati, graduating with Department Rank 1 and a CGPA of 9.7/10. He was also selected for the Government of India's prestigious international master's fellowship program associated with Heidelberg University, Germany.
