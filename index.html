Short Bio

Neelesh is a Principal Applied Scientist and Applied Sciences Lead at Oracle Generative AI, specializing in Generative AI, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), and intelligent enterprise AI systems. With over 15 years of experience spanning scientific research, applied AI, and large-scale production systems, he leads applied science and research initiatives focused on enterprise-scale AI architectures for reasoning, retrieval, NL2SQL, agentic systems, and evaluation-driven AI. His work centers on building reliable, scalable, and production-grade AI systems grounded in scientific rigor and real-world deployment.

Before joining Oracle, he spearheaded AI research and innovation at State Street, advancing the adoption of Generative AI and intelligent enterprise systems across financial services. His research focuses on Document Intelligence, Machine Reading Comprehension, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Visual NLP, learning with limited labeled data, and enterprise-grade NL2SQL systems.

He contributes to AI research community through publications and by serving as a reviewer for leading conferences and journals including ACL ARR and venues, EMNLP, AACL, and IEEE venues.

Neelesh holds an M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Guwahati, where he graduated as Department Rank 1. He was also selected for the Government of India's prestigious international master's fellowship program associated with Heidelberg University, Germany.
