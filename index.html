About Me

I'm Neelesh K Shukla, a Principal Applied Scientist at Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) Generative AI, where I lead applied sciences for GenAI and Advanced AI products. With over 14 years of experience spanning research and production, I work where research meets product — building GenAI systems backed by retrieval, evaluation, and safety that you can trust under real traffic.

Previously, I served as Sr. Manager and Data Scientist at State Street, where I incubated and led the AI Research and Innovation team, driving Generative AI adoption in the financial domain. My research focus includes Generative AI in Finance, Financial Document Understanding and Processing, and multilingual NLP.

I hold an M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Guwahati (Department Rank 1, CGPA 9.7/10), where I was part of the Open Source Intelligence Group led by Dr. S. Ranbir Singh, with a thesis on "A Study on Entity Role Detection" in attack events. I was also selected for the prestigious Govt of India's Masters' fellowship program at Heidelberg University, Germany, where I collaborated with Prof. Andrezejak on code snippet extraction, achieving the highest grade.

Current focus areas include agentic AI systems, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), multimodal and multilingual reasoning, and evaluation & safety for LLM-powered products.
