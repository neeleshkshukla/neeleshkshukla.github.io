About Me

"The best way to predict the future is to invent it." — Alan Kay

Hi — I'm Neelesh K Shukla, a Principal Applied Scientist at Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) Generative AI, where I lead applied sciences for GenAI and Advanced AI products. With over 14 years of experience spanning research and production, I work where research meets product — building GenAI systems backed by retrieval, evaluation, and safety that you can trust under real traffic.

Previously, I served as Sr. Manager and Data Scientist at State Street, where I incubated and led the AI Research and Innovation team, driving Generative AI adoption in the financial domain. I hold an M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Guwahati (Department Rank 1, CGPA 9.7/10), and was selected for the prestigious Govt of India's Masters' fellowship program at Heidelberg University, Germany.

What I'm focused on

Current focus areas include agentic AI systems, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), multimodal and multilingual reasoning, evaluation & safety, and document AI. I'm pushing an evaluation-first approach to LLM features — every surface ships with success criteria, targeted human review, and dashboards that make regressions obvious.

Why it matters

A good system shouldn't just ace a benchmark once; it should stay healthy as products evolve and edge cases appear. That's why evaluation, robustness, and safety are first-class citizens in my work, and why I publish and review — to turn lessons from production into patterns others can reuse.

Looking ahead

I'm excited about evaluation-native AI — systems that surface their confidence and trade-offs by design — and about making multilingual and multimodal experiences feel natural, not bolted on.

🪂 Off the clock: I enjoy mentoring teams building their first real AI systems and contributing to the research community through publication and review.
